## Overview

OpenAI is an AI research organization that has made foundational contributions to large language model research. Notable for the GPT series of language models and for publishing the first systematic study of neural scaling laws.

## Key Facts

- Published "Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models" (Kaplan et al., 2020), establishing power-law relationships between model performance and scale factors
- Developed the GPT series (GPT, GPT-2, GPT-3, GPT-4), which pioneered the decoder-only transformer architecture for language modeling
- Contributed Sparse Transformer for efficient attention computation
- GPT-3 uses 96 attention heads in its transformer architecture
