## Overview

Ashish Vaswani is a researcher and lead author of "Attention Is All You Need" (2017), the paper that introduced the transformer architecture. This work, conducted at Google Brain, has become one of the most cited papers in machine learning history and forms the architectural foundation of all major large language models.

## Key Facts

- Lead author of "Attention Is All You Need" (Vaswani et al., 2017) at Google Brain
- Introduced scaled dot-product attention and multi-head self-attention
- Co-authors included Noam Shazeer, Niki Parmar, Jakob Uszkoreit, Llion Jones, Aidan Gomez, Lukasz Kaiser, and Illia Polosukhin
- The transformer architecture has become the foundation for GPT, BERT, PaLM, LLaMA, and virtually all modern LLMs
